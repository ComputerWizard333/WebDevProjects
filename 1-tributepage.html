Remembering MF DOOM: Underground Hip Hop Legend

Tribute to New York Artist/Producer: Daniel Dumile aka "MF DOOM" (1971 -2020)

Remembering the world's one and only "super-villian rapper"

Who was MF DOOM?

  • He was born in 1971 to the name of Daniel Dumile
  • He grew up in Long Beach, New York but maintained British citizenship all his life because of his birth in London
  • His career as a rapper begin in 1988, at the age of 17, in the group named "KMD", under the pseudonym: Zev Love X
  • In the late 1990's he released Operation: Doomsday, about half a decade after retreating from the hip-hop scene after his brother's death
  • Daniel derived his most famous stage name, "MF DOOM", from his interest in comic books, most specifically the character Dr. Doom from the Marvel Comic Universe
  • His known aliases include: Zev Love X (as part of the group K.M.D.), King Geedorah/Ghidra (as part of the group Monsta Island Czars), Metal Fingers, Viktor Vaughn, Madvillain, DANGERDOOM, JJ DOOM, GG DOOM, MA DOOM, DILLA GHOST DOOM, DOOMStarks
